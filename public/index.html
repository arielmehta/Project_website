Our Mission

Our goal is to initiate a partnership between high school students and the Mosquito Abatement District to aid in the trapping, collecting, and identifying of vectors in our community. Along with setting up the traps, we have created this online database for members to submit their data, and have written software to analyze this data and display it in charts and tables and graphs. We aim not only to collect and analyze data, but also to tell a story with it.
